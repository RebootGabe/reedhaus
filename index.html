04 / FAQ

Asked & answered.

Do I own the website? +

You license access while you're a client. We host, maintain, and run it. If you leave, we'll hand off the content and brand assets — but the codebase and integrations stay with us. Same way you don't "own" your Squarespace site.

What if I want to cancel? +

No contracts. Cancel any month. We'll archive your data and give you 30 days to find a new home. We don't believe in hostage situations.

How fast can edits happen? +

Small edits — usually within 1–3 business days. Pro clients get 24-hour priority. Just text us. No tickets, no portals (unless you want one).

Do you do custom apps or e-commerce? +

We do simple ordering / booking / payment flows. We don't build custom web apps or full-blown Shopify-replacement stores — that's a different business. If that's you, we'll point you to someone who does that well.

Why monthly instead of one-time? +

Because "$5,000 once and you're on your own" is how most small businesses end up with a broken site, expired domain, and no Google profile two years later. We charge monthly because we're actually there every month.
